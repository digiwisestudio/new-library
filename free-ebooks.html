eBook Reading Tips

Mobile Reading

Use dedicated eBook reader apps like Kindle, Kobo, or Google Play Books on your smartphone for the best reading experience.

Eye Comfort

Enable night mode or sepia theme in your reading app during evening hours to reduce eye strain and improve sleep quality.

ePub vs PDF

ePub format offers reflowable text that adapts to your screen size, while PDF preserves exact page layouts but may require zooming on smaller devices.
